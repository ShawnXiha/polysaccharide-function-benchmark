# Journal of Cheminformatics Submission Package

## Target recommendation

- Journal: `Journal of Cheminformatics`
- Recommended article type: `Methodology`

## Why this fit

This manuscript is best framed as a reproducible computational methodology and benchmark paper rather than a pure performance-driven machine learning paper. Its strongest contribution is the construction of a practical natural-polysaccharide benchmark, a strong sparse anchor, and a structured feature analysis that identifies which signals are actually useful under current public-data conditions.

## Journal-specific requirements captured in this package

- LaTeX is accepted and Springer Nature template is encouraged.
- Main manuscript should use double spacing and line numbering.
- Figures must be uploaded in citation order; multi-panel figures should be a single composite file.
- Tables should have titles above and legends below, without color shading.
- Methodology articles require a `Scientific Contribution` section inside the abstract, maximum 3 sentences.
- Methodology articles require reproducibility: datasets, software, and algorithms needed to reach the conclusions must be provided as supplementary material or otherwise openly accessible.
- Required declarations:
  - `Availability of data and materials`
  - `Competing interests`
  - `Funding`
  - `Authors' contributions`
  - `Acknowledgements`
  - `Authors' information` (optional)
