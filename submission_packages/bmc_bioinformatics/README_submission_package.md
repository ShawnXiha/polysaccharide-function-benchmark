# BMC Bioinformatics Submission Package

## Target recommendation

- Journal: `BMC Bioinformatics`
- Recommended article type: `Research article`

## Why this fit

The manuscript is fundamentally a bioinformatics benchmark and machine-learning evaluation study on a structured biological dataset. Its strongest contribution is practical benchmark construction, reproducible baselines, and controlled feature-level analysis under weak supervision, all of which fit BMC Bioinformatics well.

## Official sources used

- Submission guidelines: <https://bmcbioinformatics.biomedcentral.com/submission-guidelines>
- General manuscript preparation: <https://bmcbioinformatics.biomedcentral.com/submission-guidelines/preparing-your-manuscript>
- Research article: <https://bmcbioinformatics.biomedcentral.com/submission-guidelines/preparing-your-manuscript/research-article>

## Journal-specific requirements captured in this package

- LaTeX is accepted and Springer Nature template is encouraged.
- Main manuscript should use double spacing and line numbering.
- Research article abstract must be structured and under 350 words:
  - `Background`
  - `Results`
  - `Conclusions`
- Required declarations:
  - `Ethics approval and consent to participate`
  - `Consent for publication`
  - `Availability of data and materials`
  - `Competing interests`
  - `Funding`
  - `Authors' contributions`
  - `Acknowledgements`
  - `Authors' information` (optional)
