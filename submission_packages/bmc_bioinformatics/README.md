## Why research article

The manuscript includes a substantial resource component, but it also contains a clean benchmark, retrieval experiments, GNN failure ablations, and ontology stability analysis. BMC Bioinformatics' database-article guidance states that an article describing a database and also including research that merits publication in its own right should either be submitted as a research article or split into separate papers.
