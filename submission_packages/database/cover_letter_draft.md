# Cover Letter Draft for Database

Dear Editors of *Database*,

We are pleased to submit the manuscript entitled **"A DoLPHiN-Derived Polysaccharide Knowledge Graph for Interpretable Function Retrieval and Tail-Sensitive Ontology Propagation"** for consideration in *Database*.

This work presents a DoLPHiN-derived polysaccharide knowledge graph that converts semi-structured records into a typed resource spanning organism, monosaccharide, glycosidic bond, function, disease, and publication evidence. Beyond resource construction, the manuscript provides a controlled retrieval benchmark and a biologically interpretable case-study analysis that clarifies what currently works on this resource and where ontology support becomes useful.

We believe the manuscript is a strong fit for *Database* because it combines three elements that align with the journal's scope: a reusable biological database resource, methodology for database normalization and graph-native benchmarking, and ontology-aware analysis relevant to biological annotation and biocuration. The manuscript also includes a concrete biological case analysis, a tail-sensitive ontology mechanism that yields a stable improvement under paired evaluation, and a separate supplementary PDF documenting case-study evidence and diagnostic ablation interpretation.

All authors approve this submission and confirm that the manuscript is original, has not been published previously, and is not under consideration elsewhere.
